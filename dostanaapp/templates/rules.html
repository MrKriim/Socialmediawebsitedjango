Rules

1. Be Kind and Respectful 🤝

Always treat others with kindness and respect. Do not engage in bullying, harassment, or hate speech. Let's create a positive and inclusive community!

2. No Spam or Self-Promotion 🚫📢

Avoid posting excessive self-promotional content or spam. Share valuable and relevant content that contributes to the community.

3. Respect Privacy 🔒

Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information or private conversations without consent. Let's create a safe and trustworthy environment.

4. No Inappropriate Content 🚫🔞

Do not post or share explicit, violent, or offensive content. Keep the platform family-friendly and suitable for users of all ages.

5. Report Violations 🚨

If you come across any violations of the rules or encounter inappropriate behavior, report it to us immediately. Together, we can maintain a positive community.

6. Comply with Laws ⚖️

Abide by all applicable laws and regulations while using Dostana. Do not engage in any activities that are illegal or violate intellectual property rights.

7. Be a Responsible User 👤

Take responsibility for your actions and contributions. Be mindful of the impact of your words and behavior on the community. Let's create a respectful and enjoyable environment for everyone.

8. Follow Instructions 📝

Follow any additional instructions or guidelines provided by the Dostana team. These guidelines are in place to ensure a smooth and positive user experience for everyone.

By using Dostana, you agree to abide by these rules. Let's foster a supportive and engaging community where everyone feels welcome and respected! 🌟😊
